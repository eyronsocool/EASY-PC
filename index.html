COMPUTER COMPONENTS

CENTRAL PROCESSING UNIT

THE BRAIN OF YOUR COMPUTER, THE CPU PROCESSES INSTRUCTIONS FROM PROGRAMS AND PERFORMS CALCULATIONS TO CARRY OUT TASKS EFFICIENTLY

MOTHERBOARD

THE FOUNDATION OF YOUR PC, THE MOTHERBOARD CONNECTS ALL COMPONENTS TOGETHER, ALLOWING THEM TO COMMUNICATE AND WORK IN HARMONY TO DELIVER OPTIMAL PERFORMANCE.

RANDOM ACCESS MEMORY

ACTING AS THE SHORT-TERM MEMORY OF YOUR COMPUTER, RAM TEMPORARILY STORES DATA THAT YOUR CPU NEEDS QUICK ACCESS TO, ENABLING SEAMLESS MULTITASKING AND FAST PERFORMANCE.

POWER SUPPLY

PROVIDING THE NECESSARY ENERGY TO ALL COMPONENTS, THE POWER SUPPLY UNIT CONVERTS ELECTRICAL POWER FROM AN OUTLET INTO USABLE POWER FOR YOUR COMPUTER.

MONITOR

YOUR WINDOW INTO THE DIGITAL WORLD, A HIGH-QUALITY MONITOR DISPLAYS CRISP VISUALS AND VIBRANT COLORS, ENHANCING YOUR COMPUTING EXPERIENCE WHETHER YOU'RE GAMING, WORKING, OR WATCHING MOVIES.

HARD DISK DRIVE

THE STORAGE POWERHOUSE OF YOUR PC, A HARD DRIVE STORES YOUR OPERATING SYSTEM, APPLICATIONS, AND PERSONAL FILES, OFFERING AMPLE SPACE FOR YOUR DIGITAL LIFE.

COOLING FAN

KEEPING YOUR SYSTEM COOL UNDER PRESSURE, A COOLING FAN DISSIPATES HEAT GENERATED BY THE CPU AND OTHER COMPONENTS, ENSURING STABLE PERFORMANCE AND LONGEVITY.

MOUSE

YOUR TRUSTY COMPANION FOR NAVIGATING THE DIGITAL REALM, A MOUSE PROVIDES PRECISION CONTROL, COMFORT, AND ERGONOMIC DESIGN TO ENHANCE YOUR PRODUCTIVITY AND GAMING EXPERIENCE.

KEYBOARD

THE KEYBOARD IS ONE OF THE MAIN WAYS TO COMMUNICATE WITH A COMPUTER. THERE ARE MANY DIFFERENT TYPES OF KEYBOARDS, BUT MOST ARE VERY SIMILAR AND ALLOW YOU TO ACCOMPLISH THE SAME BASIC TASKS.
